The Secretary of Defense initiated a Department-wide campaign to refocus attention on critical information leaks and the mishandling of classified information. All personnel are required to employ appropriate OPSEC measures in order to protect controlled unclassified information, classified national security information, pre-decisional information, and unclassified information not approved for public release.
All affiliated Joint Staff personnel are required to complete five OPSEC training modules loaded to Joint Knowledge Online (JKO) by 18 September 2020. These training modules have been preloaded into the "My Training" tab within JKO for all Joint Staff civilians and military personnel. Users who have not had the training preloaded must contact the MILSEC/JKO Training Coordinator for enrollment. Contractors will be required to search for and complete the below modules by using the search function within JKO:

— Due NLT 20 September 2020

· JS US009 – Joint Staff Operations Security (OPSEC)

· JS US072 – Joint Staff Insider Threat Awareness

· JS US078 – 2020 DoD Operations Security Campaign SECDEF Video

· JS US079 – Introduction to Information Security

· JS US080 – Unauthorized Disclosure of Classified Information for DoD and Industry
