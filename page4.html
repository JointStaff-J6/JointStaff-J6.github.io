About Data Services Division

The DSD enables warfighter access to C2 Authoritative Data Sources (ADS) and improves data interoperability by establishing common data and service standards (to include but not limited to enterprise, common mission and data access services), integrating these standards into capability requirements, and facilitating implementation within programs of record.
